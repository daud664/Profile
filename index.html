EDUCATION

L'ecole Mondiale in Lahore is an Islamic IB school that combines academic excellence with strong moral and religious values. It offers the International Baccalaureate (IB) programme, focusing on developing inquiring, knowledgeable, and caring students. The school promotes a balanced approach to education, integrating Islamic teachings with a global curriculum. The environment is welcoming, and the staff are supportive and dedicated to student success.

I have been a student at L'ecole Mondiale for the past two years, and I am currently in MYP 3. During my time here, I’ve had the opportunity to grow both academically and personally. One of the highlights of my experience has been participating in the school’s sports activities, which are taken seriously and enjoyed by many students.

Last year, I was part of the school football team, and we won a football tournament together — a memory I’m especially proud of. Overall, it’s a pretty good school where students are encouraged to explore their talents and build confidence. L'ecole Mondiale provides a great balance between modern education and Islamic values, making it a unique and enriching place to learn.
